CONFIDENTIAL_FILE :: BENSY

Privacy Policy for Bensy

Effective Date: June 19, 2026

Bensy ("we", "our", or "us") is a fuel and vehicle cost tracking application operated by BaskomLabs. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about the data the app handles.

1. Information We Collect

Account Data (Signed-in users)
  • Google Account profile: name, email address, and profile picture, obtained via Google Sign-In when you authenticate.
  • User ID: a unique identifier issued by Firebase Authentication to associate your data with your account.
Vehicle & Usage Data
  • Vehicle records: make, model, year, fuel type, and odometer readings you enter.
  • Fuel logs: date, volume, price, odometer, fuel type, and station name you record.
  • Other costs: optional records for service, parking, tolls, or related expenses.
Permission-Based Data
  • Camera: used only when you tap the camera icon to scan an odometer or receipt. Captured images are processed entirely on your device using Google ML Kit (offline OCR) to extract numbers, then discarded from temporary cache. No image content is uploaded to our servers.
  • Photo library: if you attach a receipt photo to a fuel log, only the photo you explicitly select is read by the app.
Guest Mode

If you continue as a Guest, all data stays on your device only. We do not collect, transmit, or have visibility into Guest data. Uninstalling the app or clearing app data will permanently delete it.

2. Third-Party Services

  • Firebase Authentication (Google LLC) — secure Google Sign-In.
  • Supabase — cloud database used to sync your vehicle and fuel records across your devices when signed in.
  • Google ML Kit Text Recognition — runs locally on your device. No image or text is transmitted to Google or to us.

These providers process data on our behalf and are bound by their own privacy terms.

3. How We Use Your Data

  • To provide the core functionality: storing, syncing, and analyzing your fuel and vehicle records.
  • To authenticate you and keep your account secure.
  • To display aggregate statistics (e.g. average consumption, total spend) on your own device.

We do not sell your data, use it for advertising, or share it with third parties for marketing.

4. Data Retention & Deletion

How to Delete Your Account & Data:
> Open App > Profile > Account > Delete Account.
> This permanently removes your account and all associated vehicle and fuel records from our servers.

You may also request deletion by emailing us at the address below. We will process verified requests within 30 days.

5. Children's Privacy

Bensy is not directed to children under 13 and we do not knowingly collect data from them.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The "Effective Date" above will reflect the most recent revision.

7. Contact Us

> Email: baskomdevs@gmail.com
